The Repair Process - Simple and Transparent

  1. Fill out RMA Form:
    To ensure quick processing, please fill out our Online RMA Form. A detailed description of the error significantly helps us with diagnosis and speeds up the repair process.
  2. Pack Device Securely and Send:
    Send your defective device well-packaged, ideally with a copy of the completed RMA form, to our service address: pda.repair, Repair Service, Hamburgerstr. 16, 65760 Eschborn, Germany. You bear the shipping costs to us.
  3. Incoming Inspection & Cost Estimate:
    Upon receipt of your package, your RMA request will be activated. We check the device and the described error and create a free cost estimate for the repair (except for recognized Movfast warranty cases).
  4. Repair After Approval:
    As soon as you approve the cost estimate, we will carry out the repair professionally.
  5. Return Shipping:
    After successful repair, we will send your device back to you. We cover the costs for return shipping within Germany.
  6. Status Tracking:
    Via our website, you can check the current status of your repair at any time using the RMA number or the device's serial number.
